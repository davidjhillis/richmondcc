About This Event

The annual Richmond CC Career Fair connects students and community members with employers from across the region who are actively hiring for full-time, part-time, and internship positions.

This is your opportunity to meet face-to-face with hiring managers, learn about career opportunities, and make connections that could launch your career. Whether you're graduating this semester or just exploring your options, this event is for you.

What to Expect

  • 50+ Employers: Representatives from healthcare, manufacturing, business, technology, and more
  • Resume Reviews: Free professional resume critique and improvement tips
  • On-the-Spot Interviews: Some employers will conduct interviews during the event
  • Career Advising: Speak with our career services team about your goals

How to Prepare

  1. Bring multiple copies of your resume (at least 15-20 printed copies)
  2. Dress professionally in business or business casual attire
  3. Research employers who will be attending (list provided after registration)
  4. Prepare your elevator pitch - a 30-second introduction about yourself
  5. Bring a notepad and pen to take notes during conversations
  6. Arrive early to have maximum time with employers
