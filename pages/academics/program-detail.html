Associate in Arts

Begin your journey to a bachelor's degree. Our transfer-focused Associate in Arts program provides a solid liberal arts foundation and guaranteed transfer to North Carolina universities.

2 years (60 credits)
Fall & Spring start
$76 per credit hour
On-campus & Online
Apply Now Contact Advisor Download Brochure

Program Overview

The Associate in Arts (A.A.) degree program is designed for students who plan to transfer to a four-year college or university to pursue a bachelor's degree in liberal arts fields such as education, history, English, psychology, sociology, or business.

This program provides a solid foundation in general education courses while allowing flexibility for students to explore various academic interests. Upon completion, students will have met the lower-division general education requirements at most North Carolina colleges and universities.

Through our articulation agreements with North Carolina public universities, your credits will transfer seamlessly, allowing you to enter as a junior at your chosen institution.

What You'll Learn

Critical Thinking & Analysis

Develop advanced analytical and problem-solving skills applicable across disciplines.

Effective Communication

Master written and oral communication skills essential for academic and professional success.

Cultural Awareness

Explore diverse perspectives through humanities and social science coursework.

Research Skills

Learn to locate, evaluate, and synthesize information from multiple sources.

Quantitative Reasoning

Build mathematical competency through required math and science courses.
