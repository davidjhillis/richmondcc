Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our commitment to student success and community impact.

About Us

Mission & Vision History Leadership Accreditation Campus Locations Employment

Our Mission

Richmond Community College empowers students to reach their full potential through accessible, affordable, quality education and training that prepares them for employment, transfer, and lifelong learning in a global society.

Since 1964, Richmond Community College has served as a cornerstone of educational opportunity in Richmond and Scotland counties. We are committed to providing pathways to success for traditional and non-traditional students, meeting the workforce needs of our regional employers, and fostering economic development throughout our service area.

Through innovative programs, dedicated faculty and staff, and partnerships with business and industry, we create transformative educational experiences that change lives and strengthen communities.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier community college in North Carolina, known for excellence in teaching and learning, student success, and community engagement.

Core Values

Student-Centered

We place student success at the heart of everything we do, providing personalized support and flexible learning options.

Excellence

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards in teaching, learning, and institutional effectiveness.

Integrity

We uphold ethical principles and demonstrate honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our actions.

Diversity & Inclusion

We embrace and celebrate the diversity of our students, staff, and community, fostering an inclusive environment.

Innovation

We continuously seek creative solutions and embrace new technologies to enhance learning and operations.

Community Engagement

We actively partner with community organizations to address regional needs and promote economic development.

Strategic Goals

1
Enhance Student Success

Increase completion, retention, and graduation rates through comprehensive student support services and innovative teaching strategies.

2
Strengthen Workforce Development

Align programs with regional labor market demands and expand partnerships with employers to ensure graduates are career-ready.

3
Promote Access & Equity

Remove barriers to higher education and ensure all students have equal opportunity to achieve their educational goals.

4
Foster Institutional Excellence

Invest in faculty, staff, technology, and facilities to maintain and enhance our capacity to serve students effectively.
